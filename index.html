Martha's Vineyard

Vacation Cheat Sheet
HOME BASE — 100 Whaler's Walk, Edgartown, MA 02539
⛴️ Vineyard Haven 🎠 Oak Bluffs 🎡 Edgartown 🌿 West Tisbury 🌅 Chilmark ❤️ Aquinnah 🧭 To-Do

Towns run east to west across the island — ferry in at Vineyard Haven, home base in Edgartown, sunset out at Aquinnah.

⛴️
⛴️

Vineyard Haven

Boutique shopping plus the Martha's Vineyard Museum. Usually the first stop off the ferry.

Eat Ocean Club · 9 Craft Kitchen & Bar · The Attic · Catboat Coffee · Black Dog · Art Cliff Diner

Shop India Rose Collective (BO) · Bee Blunt (BO)
🎠
🎠

Oak Bluffs

sunrise

Lively and colorful — MVAAFF, Gingerbread Cottages, Circuit Ave, Inkwell Beach, Flying Horses Carousel.

Eat Black Joy Kitchen (BO) · Biscuits (BO) · Winston's · Vineyard Caribbean Cuisine (BO) · Honorary Tavern (BO) · Donovan's Reef (BO) · Back Door Donuts · Midnight Taco · Lookout Tavern · Mocha Motts · Dos Mas · Nancy's · Aalia's Coffee · The Ritz · Noman's

Shop C'est La Vie (BO) · Island-Life Studio (BO) · Cousen Rose Gallery (BO)
🏠
🎡

Edgartown

home base · sunrise

Charming streets, boutiques, lighthouses, and classic coastal vibes.

Eat Atlantic Fish & Chop House · Bettini · Rockfish · Sweet Life Café · Behind the Bookstore · Rosewater · Atria · 19 Raw · Pelican Club · Seafood Shanty · Edgartown Diner · Toccopuro Coffee · Summer Shades

Shop Edgartown Books · The Blackdog
🌿
🌿

West Tisbury

Peaceful farms, nature preserves, and Lambert's Cove Beach — great for a slower, scenic stop.

🌅
🌅

Chilmark

sunset

Quiet, scenic, and coastal, with Menemsha fishing village views.

Eat Larsen's Fish Market · Homeport Restaurant & Oyster Bar
❤️
❤️

Aquinnah

sunset

Indigenous roots, Gay Head Light, and the stunning Aquinnah Clay Cliffs.

Eat / Stay The Outermost Inn · Orange Peel Bakery

🧭 Quick To-Do List

  • 🌉Jaws Bridge — jump in the water for a fun adventure between Oak Bluffs and Edgartown.
  • 🌅Sunrise — Inkwell Beach or Edgartown Lighthouse.
  • 🌇Sunset — Menemsha Beach or Aquinnah Cliffs Overlook.
  • 🎬MVAAFF — watch for day tickets or showings once released.
(BO) = Black-owned business · Martha's Vineyard Cheat Sheet
